About SLMYA

The Sierra Leone Muslim Youth Assembly (SLMYA) was founded to unite and empower Muslim youth across Sierra Leone, providing them with spiritual guidance, leadership training, and community service opportunities. Recognizing the challenges faced by young Muslims—such as limited access to education, social support, and leadership roles—SLMYA was established to bridge these gaps.

Since its inception, the organization has actively promoted Islamic values, youth development, and social responsibility through educational programs, community outreach, and advocacy initiatives. With a strong emphasis on unity, leadership, and service, SLMYA continues to inspire and support young Muslims to become positive change-makers in their communities and beyond.

Leadership & Structure

SLMYA has a structured leadership framework, including the President, Vice President, Secretary, and key divisions such as the Female Wing and Imam & Religious Affairs Division.
