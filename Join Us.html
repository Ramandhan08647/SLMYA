About Sierra Leone Muslim Youth Association (SLMYA)

The Sierra Leone Muslim Youth Association is a transformative initiative founded by a visionary private individual, driven by a passion to empower Muslim youth and uplift the community through education, leadership, and service.

Our Mission

To inspire, educate, and unite young Muslims across Sierra Leone through impactful programs, collaborations, and community development projects rooted in Islamic values.

Our Story & Growth

SLMYA began as a grassroots idea by a passionate youth advocate who believed in the potential of Sierra Leonean Muslim youth to lead and bring positive change. Through dedication and community engagement, the initiative evolved from small gatherings into a structured organization.

One of the most impactful steps in this journey was the strategic collaboration with institutions like Limkokwing University, which helped amplify the reach and resources of the association.

Today, SLMYA is recognized nationally for its integrity, transparency, and commitment to youth empowerment.

Our Campus & Collaborations

SLMYA continues to grow with strong ties to both local institutions and international partners, ensuring its programs remain credible and far-reaching.

Why Trust & Choose SLMYA?

With a clear vision, strong ethical foundations, and a growing record of community impact, SLMYA is widely regarded as a legitimate, trustworthy, and progressive youth movement in Sierra Leone. We combine traditional values with modern tools to mentor, educate, and inspire a new generation of confident, capable, and compassionate Muslim leaders.
